# Sam's Phones & Tobacco Website - PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a modern, mobile-friendly website for Sam's Phones & Tobacco, a local retail store in Oxford, Mississippi.

## Business Info
- **Name:** Sam's Phones & Tobacco
- **Tagline:** "If you need it, We've got it! — BUY | SELL | FIX"
- **Address:** 1418 W. Jackson Ave., Oxford, MS 38655
- **Phone:** 662-513-0004

## User Personas
1. **Local Customers** - Oxford, MS residents needing phone repairs, prepaid plans, or tobacco products
2. **Students** - Ole Miss students looking for affordable phone services
3. **Visitors** - People passing through needing quick phone fixes

## Core Requirements (Static)
- 4 pages: Home, Products & Services, Store Policies, Contact
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Dark theme with urban/street retail aesthetic
- CTA buttons for calling and directions
- Customer reviews display
- Contact form (success message only, no backend storage)
- Google Maps embed

## What's Been Implemented (December 2025)
- [x] Home page with hero section, store photos, services preview, customer reviews

- [x] Contact page with Google Map, phone, address, and contact form

- [x] Custom dark theme with electric blue (#007AFF) and neon green (#39FF14) accents
